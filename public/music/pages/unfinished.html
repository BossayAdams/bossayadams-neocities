Date Finished: 2020

This song contains my first ever key change in a song, with the bridge being in C Major while everything else is in C minor. The short intro to this track is sampled from a Warioware Touched microgame called "Domestic Issue", which was put there because I thought it sounded cool and I was too lazy to write one myself. Also, here's a warning: the synth solo at the end may hurt your ears because I made it too loud in the mix.
