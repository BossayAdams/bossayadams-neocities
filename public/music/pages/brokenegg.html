Broken Egg

This is my first ever album. I am really not proud of it, but I feel obligated to put it here for the sake of showing my progression as an artist.

Date Finished: 11/23/18

This was the first song created on this album, which, coincidentally, is also the first track. The entirety of this track is based on a simple chord progression that I got from some random chord progression generator. As for the title "Poket", it is a deliberate misspelling of "Pocket". Why? Don't know. Most of these titles don't relate to anything.
